Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1998

La Tavola Calda started as a small trattoria run by Nonna Maria and her son, Chef Antonio Bianchi. What began as a 20-seat kitchen has grown into one of the city's most beloved Italian restaurants — but the philosophy hasn't changed: use the best ingredients, cook with love, and treat every guest like family.

Our pasta is made fresh every morning. Our sauces simmer for hours. Our bread comes out of the oven minutes before it reaches your table. This isn't fast food — it's food made the right way.
